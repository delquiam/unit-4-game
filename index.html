Crystals Collector!

INSTRUCTIONS

The game will start when you click on a crystal. Click on one of the four crystals. Once you click on a crystal, you will be given a random number. The object of the game is to match the random number.

After the random number is given, each crystal will be worth a certain amount of points. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on a crystal.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time a new game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
